Hi — I'm **[Le Duc Loc]**, a developer who learns best by doing and writing.

This blog is my public notebook. Instead of keeping notes locked away in private docs, I publish them here. That accountability helps me think more clearly, and occasionally it helps someone else too.

## What you'll find here

- **Tutorials & walkthroughs** — step-by-step guides for things I've actually built or broken
- **Concept deep dives** — my attempt to explain something until I truly understand it
- **Tool notes** — honest impressions of libraries, languages, and dev tools
- **Learning logs** — rough notes from courses, books, and experiments

## Why "Learning in Public"

I don't wait until I'm an expert to write. Most posts are written from *just past the point of confusion* — which I think is the most useful place to write from. If you spot a mistake, [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/imnotwannafire/imnotwannafire.github.io/issues) or reach out directly.

## Stack

This blog runs on [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) + [Minimal Mistakes](https://mmistakes.github.io/minimal-mistakes/) and is hosted on GitHub Pages. No trackers, no ads.
